Milk Farm Tycoon

  • Featured Skills

During my time at NoPowerUp, I was entrusted with multiple responsibilities ranging from UI/Gameplay programmer, bridge engineer, and intern supervisor. I also gained a fair amount of experience working in game design, game testing and tool programming.

  • My experience

Working at NoPowerUp was absolute bliss for me. Despite being a larger-than-average company, it does feel like a big family there, everyone was really friendly and knowledgeable. Milk Farm Tycoon was my very first project at NPU. The moment I finished my internship, I was instantly included in the pre-production team for Milk Farm Tycoon where I had to analyze and implement a complex codebase given by our overseas partner. More than 50% of the game UI was put together by me, this includes every upgrade popup, quest indicators, dialogue/tutorial scenes and time-limited events. I also enjoy working on gameplay features such as the Cow Behaviour System, Player Statistic System and the Lootbox system. I was the go-to guy for almost every bug in the game. My most memorable bug was when due to a change in Unity, our game occasionally fell into soft-lock from time to time and no one on the tester team could accurately replicate it but within half a day, I successfully pinpointed and resolved the bug for good.
